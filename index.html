HI

St. Joseph’s International School has proudly celebrated 6 years of delivering a world-class education system in March 2021. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), we’re a day boarding and residential school. Our 17-acre campus consisting of the best infrastructure, technology and faculty, is located just 12km from Hisar on the Hisar-Sirsa Road.

St. Joseph’s has welcomed students from all backgrounds. We encourage students to nurture their talents and to create relationships that are bound to last a lifetime. From Nursery to 12th Standard, we have a full enrollment capacity of 2,500 students.

We provide full-boarding facilities of the highest standard for students to make them feel at home. Our GPS equipped buses ensure that each child staying in localities of Hisar or nearby towns has a safe mode of transportation to- and from the campus.

At St. Joseph’s, we aim to create an environment where each child feels welcomed and encouraged. We’re a multicultural school that aims to form habits that will serve them even after their time with us. By taking individual and collective responsibility of the children under our care, we hope to create an environment where every child feels like learning.

teachers

At St. Joseph’s International School we can proudly say ,we have the best teachers in our faculty with years of experience in teaching, guiding and counselling students, our teachers are proud members of the family that is St. Joseph’s.

All members of our faculty have one goal: improvement. And with that at the forefront of the mind, our teachers take personal interest in each student.They interact with them individually. Whether it is in the form of an extra class or an individual counselling session, each teacher focuses solely on the students.

We encourage individual teacher-parent interaction so that each parent not only understands his ward better but can convey any problems or worries to the teacher directly.
